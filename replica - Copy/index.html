Who We Are

Our Mission

Empowering women in cybersecurity by providing accessible education and hands-on training to build a diverse, resilient workforce in the digital security field.

Our Vision

To create an inclusive cybersecurity community where women can thrive, innovate, and lead the way in safeguarding information globally.

Our Objectives

  • Equip young women with critical cybersecurity skills
  • Foster a supportive learning environment
  • Increase representation in tech fields
  • Build awareness of diversity in digital security

History and Impact

Since its inception, Cyber Girls has been dedicated to bridging the gender gap in tech. Through workshops, mentorship, and resources, we have enabled hundreds of young women to pursue cybersecurity careers, impacting both the industry and local communities.

Our Story

Program Details

The Cyber Girls program equips participants with essential skills in cybersecurity. Below are the key topics covered:

  • Network Security
    Learn about firewalls, VPNs, and network protocols to protect digital information and secure communication channels.
  • Ethical Hacking
    Gain hands-on experience in penetration testing and vulnerability assessment to detect and mitigate security risks.
  • Data Protection
    Understand the principles of data privacy and protection, including encryption, access control, and compliance.

Duration: 12 weeks

Format: Online and in-person sessions

Prerequisites: Basic computer skills

How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to enroll in our program. We look forward to guiding you through a rewarding journey in cybersecurity!

  1. Check Eligibility: Applicants must be 18 years or older with a basic understanding of computer systems.
  2. Gather Documents: Prepare an updated resume, proof of identification, and any relevant certifications.
  3. Submit Application: Complete the online form below or visit our application portal.
  4. Application Deadline: Applications are due by December 15, 2024.
